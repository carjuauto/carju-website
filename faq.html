Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about deposits, timelines, auctions, shipping, and payments.

We require a ¥150,000 auction deposit to activate bidding. If you win and pay within 3 business days, we credit the deposit to your final invoice. If you win but fail to pay, auction houses may retain the deposit per their policy. This also affects the reputation of our company so please stick to the agreement.

Tell us your target vehicle and budget. We shortlist units using real auction sheets and photos, then bid on your approval. If we win the bid, we handle payment, de-registration, export docs, and Shipping booking and Loading.

Typical lead time is 6-10 weeks to East Africa depending on sailing schedules and port congestion. We’ll keep you updated from purchase to Bill of Lading (B/L).

Our agency fees are transparent and tiered by purchase price. See the full breakdown on the Fees page. No hidden charges.

Payment is via bank transfer to our corporate account in Japan. Installments are not available for winning bids, but we can discuss phased purchasing for multiple units.
